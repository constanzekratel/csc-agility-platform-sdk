Workflow Policy

Workflow policy can be used to govern the service instance lifecycle events. Agility Platformprovides the following workflow scopes by default, which can be customized according to organizational requirements:

■ Service Delete
■ Service Provision
■ Service Release
■ Service Start
■ Service Stop
■ Service Restart
